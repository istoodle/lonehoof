how to buy

Ordering is straightforward. There's no checkout — just a short exchange between us.

1

Get in touch

Drop me a message via the contact page or on Instagram. Just tell me which print you'd like.

2

I send you a PayPal link

I'll reply with a PayPal payment link for the correct amount, including any postage. No PayPal account needed — you can pay by card through PayPal's guest checkout.

3

You pay

Once payment is confirmed, I'll ask for your delivery address if I don't already have it.

4

I place the order

I place the order with The Print Space (via The Creative Hub) — a professional print and fulfilment studio with locations in the UK and mainland Europe. They handle printing, packaging, and shipping directly to your door.

5

Your print arrives

The Print Space ship using eco-friendly, gallery-standard packaging. You'll receive tracking information and your print — along with a certificate of authenticity — should arrive within a few days.

About the prints

Each print is a strictly limited edition of 10. All are signed and numbered, and come with a certificate of authenticity.

The originals are charcoal on paper. The timeless depth of that medium is reproduced as a fine art Giclée print on Hahnemühle German Etching paper — a favourite among artists and collectors for its rich texture and archival quality.

PaperHahnemühle German Etching, 310 gsm
FinishMatt, slightly textured — a classic hand-crafted feel
InksArchival pigment inks, fade-resistant for 100+ years
EditionStrictly limited to 10, signed & numbered
ShippingVia The Print Space for sustainable, gallery-standard production and delivery
