Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Lone Hoof collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users of the lonehoof.com website ("Site").

Personal identification information

We may collect personal identification information from users in a variety of ways, including when users visit our site, place an order, or fill out a form. Users may be asked for their name, email address, or mailing address. Users may visit the site anonymously. We will collect personal identification information only if voluntarily submitted.

Non-personal identification information

We may collect non-personal information about users whenever they interact with our site. This may include browser name, computer type, and technical information about the means of connection to our site.

Email subscriptions

If you subscribe to the mailing list, we collect your email address and, if provided, your name. This is used to send occasional Lone Hoof news. Emails are only sent to those who have explicitly opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email. The legal basis for processing this data is your consent.

Your data protection rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to request access to personal data we hold about you, request corrections, or ask for your data to be deleted. You also have the right to withdraw consent for email communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link or contacting us directly.

Third-party services

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Orders are processed manually via PayPal. Personal data submitted through contact forms is sent directly to us via your email client and is not stored on any third-party server by this site. Shipping is fulfilled by The Print Space; buyer delivery addresses are passed to them solely for order fulfilment.

Web browser cookies

Our site may use cookies to enhance user experience. You may choose to set your browser to refuse cookies, though some parts of the site may not function properly if you do so.

How we use collected information

Lone Hoof uses personal information to process orders, respond to enquiries, and (where opted in) send occasional news updates. We do not share personal information with outside parties except as necessary to fulfil orders.

How we protect your information

We adopt appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorised access or disclosure of your personal information. Payment processing is handled by PayPal, who comply with relevant security standards.

Third-party websites

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

Changes to this privacy policy

Lone Hoof has the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. We encourage users to check this page periodically for changes.

Contacting us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at lone_hoof@outlook.com.
