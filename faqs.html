Do you sell prints or originals?

I've sold originals in the past but right now I am only selling selected prints. I use The Print Space (via The Creative Hub), a professional drop-shipping platform based in the UK and mainland Europe. When you order a print, they produce and ship it directly to you using top-tier print tech and eco-friendly packaging. I've done a drop-ship test of all the prints I'm selling and it's very impressive: the fidelity of the print, the quality of the paper, the packaging, the tracking. All of it.

What's the print quality?

They are fine art Giclée prints on Hahnemühle German Etching paper. Top quality!

Do you sign your prints?

I don't sign them (they're shipped to you directly) but they do come with a digitally signed certificate of authenticity.

What sizes are available?

All dimensions and other details can be found on each listed product. If you have any other questions, please get in touch.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship across Europe. One of the reasons I chose The Creative Hub is that, because of their UK and mainland European locations, they can keep shipping costs in check — and no customs headaches either.

How do I order?

Drop me a message via the contact page or on Instagram. Tell me what you'd like and I'll send you a PayPal invoice. Once paid, I'll place the order with The Print Space and they'll ship directly to you.
